Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work with YouTube Premium?

Yes! CinemaTube Pro works seamlessly with YouTube Premium and enhances your premium experience even further.

Can I customize the appearance?

Absolutely! Choose from 5 themes, adjust opacity, video size, glow effects, and enable ambient mode.

Will it slow down my browser?

No. CinemaTube Pro is lightweight and optimized for performance with minimal resource usage.

Does it work on mobile?

Currently, CinemaTube Pro is available for desktop browsers only. Mobile support is planned for future updates.

Never. CinemaTube Pro doesn't track, collect, or share any of your data. Your privacy is 100% protected.
